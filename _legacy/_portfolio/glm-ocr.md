## Problem
Legal scans are hostile to off-the-shelf OCR: multi-column layouts, stamps, fringe artifacts, and domain vocabulary.

## Approach
A dedicated `glm_ocr` workspace with:

- Fine-tuning scripts aimed at document understanding models
- Inference utilities for PDF inputs
- Example materials drawn from German civil-chamber style documents

## Why it matters
Shows willingness to leave clean academic datasets and wrestle with **real document noise** — a frequent requirement in applied NLP roles.
